Simple Hangout Generator with Google Maps

Are you tired of your girlfriend or wife never knowing where she wants to go?
Same.
I built this little thing in order to solve that problem. (and for my portfolio)

Only show open places:

Click "Toggle Map" then anywhere on the map to pick a location, or search for an address instead.
Modify some options from the "Toggle Controls" button.
Click "Find" to get a random location to go to.
